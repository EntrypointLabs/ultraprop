## Anti-Patterns Verdict

**Does this look AI-generated? No.** Distinctive, cohesive, restrained after the quieter pass.

**Deterministic scan:** `detect.mjs` over `app/` + `components/` returned `[]` (exit 0). Zero slop tells — no gradient text, no eyebrow scaffolding, no side-stripe borders, no over-rounded cards, no ghost-card shadow pairs, no repeating-stripe backgrounds.

**But it still looks like a crypto app — the user's #1 anti-reference.** The neon/pixel layer is gone (good), yet the *vocabulary and identifiers* still advertise the chain: "SBT", "v1 Genesis SBT", "recorded on-chain", "Object ID", "View SBT on Sui Explorer", "Verifiable on-chain", raw `0x…` addresses under every handle, the "SUI" chain chip, "oracle prices", protocol names (Pyth, 7K, Cetus/Aftermath/Turbos/Kriya), and "10X" leverage chips. The chain is supposed to be plumbing; right now the surface keeps pointing at it. This is the dominant finding.

## Overall Impression

The quieter pass worked: one calm indigo accent, quiet gray leverage chips, restrained CTAs, a dark institutional share panel, softened confetti. The bones are excellent — the slippage-transparency ticket, the equity-curve cockpit, the consistent tables. The single biggest opportunity is now **language, not color**: take the crypto out of the words and the identifiers the way we just took it out of the palette. That one move closes most of the gap to "institutional, not crypto."

## What's Working

- **The trust primitive.** The fill-preview ticket (market mid → size slippage → +2 bps spread → your fill, routed via 7K) is genuinely best-in-class transparency. Keep it; only soften the protocol naming.
- **Cohesion.** Shared primitives give every screen the same button, table, pill, and badge language. Detector-clean. This is hard to achieve and it's here.
- **State coverage.** Loading skeletons, three distinct terminal screens, the stale-feed halt banner, the dignified failure debrief. Most apps ship half of these.

## Priority Issues

- **[P1] The surface still speaks crypto.** Violates Design Principle 1 (the chain is plumbing) and the primary anti-reference.
  - *Why it matters:* the institutional trader you're courting reads "SBT / 0x / Sui Explorer / oracle" as crypto-casino signals and discounts trust — the exact opposite of the goal.
  - *Fix:* rename in the UI — "SBT/credential", "Genesis credential" not "Genesis SBT"; lead with handles (satoshi.sui) and demote raw `0x…` to a copy-on-click detail; fold the "SUI" chip + "Sui Explorer" + "Object ID" into one quiet "Verify ↗" affordance; say "market price" with "Pyth/7K" as drill-in detail; reconsider "10X" → "10× buying power" or just the allocation. Keep on-chain verifiability available, never as the headline.
  - *Suggested command:* `$impeccable clarify`
- **[P1] Muted-gray legibility risks AA.** `--text-faint #6b6b73` on `--bg #0a0a0c` is ≈3.6:1, under the 4.5:1 body floor.
  - *Why it matters:* secondary labels ("Shadow size", "of 200 intent cap", footnotes, the `0x…a5e2` sub-rows on the leaderboard, "Joined Apr 23") are hard to read; fails the WCAG AA target you set.
  - *Fix:* lift `--text-faint` toward `#8a8a93`+ (or only use it for ≥18px/bold), and verify placeholder + on-tint text. Confirm with a contrast pass.
  - *Suggested command:* `$impeccable audit`
- **[P2] Home stacks four competing heavy sections.** GENESIS banner + active-eval card + markets table + tier ladder, each a large card, all full-weight.
  - *Why it matters:* a returning trader's primary task (resume / trade) competes with the marketing banner and the tier ladder; nothing clearly wins the eye.
  - *Fix:* establish one primary section by state (active eval → cockpit-forward; new user → tier ladder/CTA forward), demote the rest; tighten vertical rhythm so the page isn't four equal slabs.
  - *Suggested command:* `$impeccable layout`
- **[P2] Help is a dead end.** "How it works?" just reopens onboarding; the Docs nav item has no destination; there's no FAQ / "what is paper trading / how do fills work / is there a token" content (A16 in the plan).
  - *Why it matters:* first-timers and skeptics have nowhere to resolve doubt; hurts activation and the trust posture.
  - *Fix:* wire Docs to real content (even a single help page), give "How it works?" a short explainer, add the FAQ.
  - *Suggested command:* `$impeccable harden`
- **[P3] No power-user accelerators.** No keyboard shortcuts, no command palette, no bulk actions for an audience that lives in trading tools.
  - *Suggested command:* later (`$impeccable craft command palette`)

## Persona Red Flags

**Sam (accessibility-dependent):** `text-faint` secondary labels and the leaderboard `0x…` sub-rows fall under 4.5:1. Unlabeled icon controls (gift, footer socials, the user/profile icon). Good news: P&L always pairs color with a sign/label, and focus rings are present.

**Alex (power user):** No keyboard shortcuts or command palette; sortable tables + quick-size presets help, but the core "submit intent" loop is mouse-first. No bulk/close-all on positions.

**The wary institutional trader (project persona):** Opens the app, sees "v1 Genesis SBT", a `0x9a4f…` address, "View on Sui Explorer", and "10X" chips — and pattern-matches it to a crypto-points-farm, not a prop desk. The vocabulary is the trust leak.

## Minor Observations

- "Shadow PnL / Shadow size / Shadow allocation" — "shadow" is internal jargon; consider "simulated balance" / "buying power".
- Profile "Object ID" row is pure chain detail in the foreground.
- The GENESIS outlined display header (points) is calmer at 48px but still a stylized treatment; fine, watch it doesn't drift loud.
- Confetti still fires on pass (softened + reduced-motion-guarded) — appropriate.

## Questions to Consider

- If you deleted every `0x…` address and chain name from the surface, would anything a trader needs actually break — or just the crypto signaling?
- What's the one number on the home screen a returning trader came to see, and is it the loudest thing right now?
- Should "credential" replace "SBT" everywhere, with on-chain proof living behind a single "Verify" link?
