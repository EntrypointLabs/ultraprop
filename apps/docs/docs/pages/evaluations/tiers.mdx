# Tiers & accounts

A **tier** sets four things about your evaluation: how large your account is, how
much profit you need to pass, how much room you have to lose, and how much leverage
you can use. You choose your tier before you start, and the full ruleset is shown
before you commit: nothing is hidden behind a click.

## The live tiers (closed beta)

| Tier | Account size | Profit target | Max drawdown | Daily loss limit | Trade budget | Max leverage |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Starter** | $10,000 | 8% | 10% | 5% | 200 | 10× |
| **Basic** | $25,000 | 8% | 8% | 5% | 200 | 8× |
| **Pro** | $50,000 | 10% | 8% | 5% | 200 | 8× |

- **Account size** is *notional*: a number the firm carries for you. In the closed
  beta no real capital backs it; in the funded phase, real vault capital does, while
  your balance stays notional. See [the funded phase](/funded/overview).
- **Profit target** is the gain that passes the tier.
- **Max drawdown** and **daily loss limit** are the two hard rules that can fail you.
- **Max leverage** is a per-tier cap; an individual market may cap you lower. See
  [Markets & instruments](/evaluations/markets).

Read [The rules](/evaluations/rules) for exactly how each limit is measured.

## The tier ladder

Tiers unlock in order. You start at **Starter**; passing it unlocks **Basic**, and
passing Basic unlocks **Pro**. Higher tiers carry a larger account and a stronger
[credential level](/record/credential), and rank higher on the
[leaderboard](/record/leaderboard).

```
Starter  →  Basic  →  Pro
 $10k        $25k      $50k
```

You hold one active evaluation account at a time. Passing a tier records the pass on
your credential and opens the next tier's evaluation.

:::

| Tier | Eval fee | Account size | Profit target | Max drawdown | Profit split (you / firm) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Starter | $100 | $10,000 | 8% | 10% | 75 / 25 |
| Basic | $250 | $25,000 | 8% | 10% | 80 / 20 |
| Pro | $500 | $50,000 | 8% | 10% | 80 / 20 |
| Elite | $1,000 | $100,000 | 10% | 8% | 85 / 15 |
| Whale | $2,500 | $250,000 | 10% | 8% | 90 / 10 |

Where the live-beta numbers above differ from this v2 table (for example Basic's
drawdown), the **live tier table is what your current evaluation uses.** The v2 table
is the forward design and will be confirmed when the funded phase ships.

## Choosing a tier

- Starting out, or want the most room to make mistakes? **Starter** has the widest
  drawdown band.
- Confident and want a larger account? **Basic** or **Pro** raise the account size
  but tighten the drawdown to 8%.
- Every tier shares the same 5% daily loss limit and 200-trade budget, so the trade
  cadence is the same across all three.

If a funded account is later terminated, you re-enter at any tier you choose by
purchasing a new evaluation: same tier, lower, or higher. See
[Passing & failing](/evaluations/outcomes).
