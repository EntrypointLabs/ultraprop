# Roadmap

Ultraprop ships in phases. The closed beta proves the evaluation; later phases add real
capital, more ways to trade, and more markets. This page is an honest map of what's
live, what's next, and what's further out, not a set of promises about returns or
dates.

:::info
**Status legend.** **Live** is in the product today. **Next** is actively being
built. **Planned** is on the roadmap and scoped, but not yet started.
:::

## Live today (v1 closed beta)

- **The evaluation**: paper-trading against live market prices with the full risk
  engine enforcing [the rules](/evaluations/rules).
- **Three tiers**: [Starter, Basic, Pro](/evaluations/tiers).
- **The Hyperliquid perpetuals catalog**: ~180 markets, per-market leverage, cross and
  isolated margin. See [Markets](/evaluations/markets).
- **Market orders with take-profit / stop-loss brackets.** See [Placing trades](/evaluations/trading).
- **The [Genesis credential](/record/credential), [leaderboard](/record/leaderboard),
  and [public profiles](/record/profile).**
- **Light and dark themes.**

## Next

- **Live mark & funding fidelity**: streaming venue marks and funding rates, accurate
  per-market fees, and precise liquidation pricing, so the catalog behaves exactly like
  the live venue.
- **Resting order types**: limit and stop-entry orders, beyond today's market-order
  entries and bracket exits.

## Planned

### The funded phase (v2)

Real capital, [profit splits](/funded/splits), [payouts](/funded/payouts), and the
**Elite** and **Whale** tiers. This is the big one: it turns a proven track record
into a real-capital account. See [The funded phase](/funded/overview) for the full
forward spec.

### More venues

A second perpetuals venue (**Bybit**) and spot execution (**Cetus**) alongside the
funded phase, with **Suilend** used only to earn yield on idle vault capital. The aim
is one consistent trading surface across more liquidity.

### New trading categories

The PRD scopes additional categories as first-class evaluation surfaces once real
settlement exists for them:

- **Prediction markets**: taking positions on event outcomes (the Polymarket-style
  category). These need real settlement against an external outcome, so they arrive with
  the real-capital phases, not paper trading.
- **Airdrop strategies**: using funded capital to run the qualifying on-chain
  interactions that protocols reward, with the platform taking a cut of what's claimed.
  This can't be meaningfully paper-traded, so it's a funded-phase category.

Both are explicitly **out of scope for the closed beta** and gated behind the
infrastructure that makes them real.

### A portable reputation credential

A production reputation system that other protocols can read, so your verified track
record can unlock access, better terms, or higher limits elsewhere. The closed-beta
[Genesis credential](/record/credential) is the seed of this; the production schema
comes once the use cases are visible.

## Further out (ecosystem)

Directional, not committed: a public capital vault where outside capital can back the
funded pool; community governance over evaluation parameters; a partner program for
protocols building on trader reputation; and expansion to additional chains and venues
as liquidity justifies it.

:::warning
**No token, and no airdrop promise.** Nothing on this roadmap is a token, an
allocation, or a reward beyond the [profit splits](/funded/splits) described for the
funded phase. The Genesis credential is proof of trading skill, not a claim on future
value. See [the credential](/record/credential).
:::
