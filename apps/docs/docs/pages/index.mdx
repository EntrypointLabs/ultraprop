# What is Ultraprop

Ultraprop is a **proprietary trading firm built for crypto traders.** You prove your
skill in a rule-bound evaluation against live market prices, and a verifiable,
on-chain record of how you traded follows you afterward. There is no capital
ceiling imposed by your own bankroll — the firm provides the capital; you provide
the edge.

The model is the one traditional prop firms have run for years (FTMO, Topstep, and
the like): pay a small evaluation fee, hit a profit target without breaking the
risk rules, and graduate to a funded account where profits are split with the firm.
Ultraprop applies that model to crypto markets and improves it with what a public
blockchain natively offers — **transparent track records, automatic rule
enforcement, and global, near-instant settlement.**

:::info
**Where the product is today.** Ultraprop is in **v1 closed beta** — an
invite-led, paper-trading phase. You trade against live market prices with the full
risk engine running, but no real capital is at stake and there are no payouts yet.
v1 exists to validate the evaluation and build a trader cohort before real money
goes live in **v2 (the funded phase)**. Everything marked _funded_ or _v2_ in these
docs is the forward plan, not a promise of a return.
:::

## The three things that make it different

- **The math is shown, never hidden.** Before any trade is submitted, you see the
  exact fill you will get — market price, the house tilt, and fees, broken out line
  by line. See [The fill & slippage model](/transparency/fill-model).
- **Rules are enforced by code, not by a reviewer.** Drawdown, daily loss, profit
  target, and trade budget are checked by the evaluation contract. There is no
  back-office override and no appeal — the outcome is whatever the rules decided.
  See [On-chain rule enforcement](/transparency/enforcement).
- **Your record is yours, and it is verifiable.** Every pass, every credential, and
  every relevant trade is recorded on-chain, so your track record is something you
  can prove to anyone — not a screenshot from a firm's private database. See
  [The Genesis credential](/record/credential).

## How a trader moves through Ultraprop

1. **Sign up.** Email or social login. An account is created for you behind the
   scenes — no wallet setup, no seed phrase, no crypto knowledge required.
2. **Pick a tier.** [Starter, Basic, or Pro](/evaluations/tiers) — each sets your
   account size, profit target, and risk limits. The full ruleset is shown before
   you commit.
3. **Pass the evaluation.** [Trade the markets](/evaluations/markets) and hit your
   profit target without breaking a [rule](/evaluations/rules).
4. **Build your record.** Passing levels up your non-transferable
   [Genesis credential](/record/credential) and moves you up the
   [leaderboard](/record/leaderboard).
5. **Get funded (v2).** When the funded phase goes live, passing earns a
   [real-capital account](/funded/overview) with a profit split and
   [payouts](/funded/payouts).

## Who it's for

Skilled crypto traders who are capped by their own capital — the trader running
$2,000 of personal funds whose 100% year is still only $2,000. Particularly those
shut out of traditional prop firms by geography, banking rails, or KYC friction,
and who already trade the assets Ultraprop offers.
