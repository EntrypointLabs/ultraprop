# Leaderboard

The leaderboard ranks the trader cohort by what they've demonstrably done. Like
everything else on Ultraprop, it's driven by on-chain records, so a position on it is
something you earned and anyone can check.

## How traders are ranked

You can sort the board three ways:

- **Highest tier reached**: Pro outranks Basic outranks Starter. The cleanest measure
  of how far a trader has climbed.
- **Simulated P&L**: total profit and loss across the trader's evaluations.
- **Total passes**: how many evaluations the trader has cleared.

The top three are shown on a podium; the rest follow in a ranked table (and a card
layout on mobile).

## Time windows

The board has two views:

- **This cohort**: the current weekly window, so a strong recent run shows even if
  you're new.
- **All-time**: the full record since the beta opened.

## Cohort stats

Above the board, a set of cohort-wide stats gives context for the whole beta group:
member count, active evaluations, total passes, the **pass rate**, and the **median
passer return**. These are the same aggregate numbers the firm watches to keep the
evaluation honest. If the pass rate drifts, the rules get re-examined. Showing them
publicly is part of the [transparency stance](/transparency/verifiability).

## Identity on the board

Traders appear by their account handle, and each entry carries their current
[credential tier](/record/credential). Click through to a trader's
[public profile](/record/profile) for their full record.

:::tip
Climbing the leaderboard and leveling your credential are the same motion: clear
higher tiers, trade consistently, and your standing follows. There's no separate
grind and no points to farm.
:::
