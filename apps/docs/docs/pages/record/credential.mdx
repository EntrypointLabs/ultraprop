# The Genesis credential

When you pass your first evaluation, Ultraprop issues you a **Genesis credential** — a
single, non-transferable record of what you've proven as a trader. It is the closest
thing the platform has to an identity: not a username you picked, but a record of what
you actually did.

## What it is

- **One per trader.** Issued once, on your first pass. It is never re-issued and never
  duplicated.
- **Non-transferable.** It can't be sold, listed, lent, or moved to another account.
  It means something precisely because you can't acquire it any way other than earning
  it.
- **It levels up in place.** Rather than collecting a new badge each time, your one
  credential levels up as you clear higher tiers.
- **It's on-chain and verifiable.** Anyone can independently check it — it isn't a
  claim sitting in a private database. See [Verifiability](/transparency/verifiability).

## How it levels up

| Level | Earned by |
| --- | --- |
| Unranked | New account, no pass yet |
| Starter | Passing the Starter evaluation |
| Basic | Passing the Basic evaluation |
| Pro | Passing the Pro evaluation |

The credential also carries a running record of your activity — your highest tier,
total passes, total simulated P&L, total trades, and when you were last active —
which accumulates across every attempt, passes and fails alike.

## What it proves

The credential is **proof of skill, demonstrated under enforced rules** — nothing
more and nothing less. Specifically, it attests that the holder:

- Traded against **live market prices**, not a delayed or synthetic feed.
- Was held to **automatic rule enforcement** with no possibility of a back-office
  override.
- Earned every level **once, per account**, with the credential non-transferable.
- Did so under a **disclosed, identical slippage model** — the same +2 bps house tilt
  applied to everyone.

That's the whole value. The credential is a verifiable trading résumé you can point
anyone to.

:::warning
**No token. No promise of a token.** The Genesis credential is not an allocation, a
points balance redeemable for anything, or a claim on future value. It is a record of
proven trading skill, and that is all it is or implies. Ultraprop makes no promise of
rewards beyond the profit splits described for the [funded phase](/funded/splits).
:::

## Why "Genesis"

The closed-beta cohort is the first group of traders inside Ultraprop. Their
credentials are the genesis records of the platform — minted once, never transferable,
and carried forward as the platform grows. The credential is designed to **evolve in
place** into the funded phase, gaining real-capital history without ever being
re-issued, so an early, hard-won record stays a record.
