# FAQ

## Getting in

### Do I need to know anything about crypto?

No. You sign up with email or a social login, and an account is created for you behind
the scenes: no wallet, no seed phrase, no gas, no network settings. See
[Verifiability](/transparency/verifiability) for what's happening underneath, if you're
curious.

### Is there KYC?

The closed beta is invite-led with no fiat banking surface, so there's no document
upload or KYC theatre to get started. Lightweight verification may be added later only
where a jurisdiction requires it.

### How do I get access right now?

Ultraprop is in **closed beta.** Starter evaluations open via an invite code; Basic and
Pro are started by paying the [evaluation fee](/evaluations/payment).

## The evaluation

### What exactly do I have to do to pass?

Reach your tier's [profit target](/evaluations/rules) (8% or 10%) without breaking a
hard rule: your maximum drawdown or your daily loss limit. That's it.

### What ends my evaluation early?

Crossing your **maximum drawdown** or your **daily loss limit**. Either one freezes the
account immediately. There are no warnings and no appeals. See
[Passing & failing](/evaluations/outcomes).

### Can I get the fee back if I fail?

No. The evaluation fee is non-refundable per attempt. In the [funded phase](/funded/payouts),
your *first successful payout* refunds your original fee, but that applies to funded
trading, not to a failed paper evaluation.

### What can I trade?

Crypto **perpetuals**: the Hyperliquid catalog of ~180 markets, long or short, with
per-market leverage and cross or isolated margin. See [Markets](/evaluations/markets).
Spot, prediction markets, and airdrop strategies are on the [roadmap](/roadmap).

### What order types are there?

Market-order entries with optional **take-profit / stop-loss brackets** (which can
carry an expiry). Resting limit and stop-entry orders are on the
[roadmap](/roadmap). See [Placing trades](/evaluations/trading).

## Fairness & trust

### Who decides if I passed or failed?

The contract does. Rules are [enforced in code](/transparency/enforcement); there's no
human reviewer, no override, and nothing to appeal. The outcome is whatever the rule
computed from your trades, and you can verify it yourself.

### Is the slippage hidden?

No. Your exact fill (oracle price, size slippage, the +2 bps house tilt, and the
taker fee) is shown line by line **before** you submit. See
[The fill & slippage model](/transparency/fill-model).

### What happens if prices break mid-trade?

If the price feed goes stale, trading **pauses** rather than filling you against a bad
number. See [Prices & oracles](/transparency/prices).

### Why does the daily limit reset at a weird time for me?

The daily loss limit resets at **00:00 UTC** for everyone; there's no per-trader
timezone. The cockpit shows a live countdown to the next reset.

## The record

### What's the Genesis credential: is it a token or an airdrop?

**Neither.** It's a non-transferable, on-chain record of what you've proven as a
trader: proof of skill, not an allocation or a claim on future value. Ultraprop makes
no token or airdrop promise. See [The Genesis credential](/record/credential).

### Does a failed attempt wipe my record?

No. Your credential accumulates history across every attempt: passes, fails, and
inactive terminations all stay part of the record.

## Money (funded phase / v2)

### When do real payouts start?

In the **funded phase (v2)**, which is not yet live. The closed beta is paper-trading
with no real capital and no payouts. See [The funded phase](/funded/overview).

### How big is the profit split?

From **75/25** up to **90/10** in your favor, depending on tier, and it improves as you
stay profitable. See [Profit splits & scaling](/funded/splits).

### How fast are payouts?

Stablecoin transfers settle in minutes and reach anywhere, with an automatic fiat
off-ramp: no wire transfer, no bank rejection. See [Payouts](/funded/payouts).
