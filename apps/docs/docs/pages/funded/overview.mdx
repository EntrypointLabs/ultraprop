# From evaluation to funded

:::warning
**The funded phase is v2: the real-capital phase, not yet live.** Today's closed beta
is paper-trading only: no real capital, no payouts. This section documents the forward
plan so you know where the platform is going. Numbers and policies marked _open_ will
be confirmed before the funded phase ships.
:::

## What "funded" means

Pass an evaluation in the funded phase and you graduate to a **funded account** backed
by the firm's real capital. You trade the same surface you trained on, same cockpit,
same markets, same rules, but now:

- **Real vault capital backs your trading.** Your balance is still *notional* (you
  never custody the firm's money), but real capital is deployed behind your positions
  on mainnet venues.
- **Profits are split** with the firm and **paid out** in stablecoins. See
  [Profit splits & scaling](/funded/splits) and [Payouts](/funded/payouts).
- **The rules still bind.** Breaking a hard rule **terminates** the funded account.

## How execution changes

In the closed beta, the platform contract is the only place a trade happens; every
rule can be checked before a fill. In the funded phase, trades execute on **real
venues** (spot on Cetus, perpetuals on Bluefin). That changes *when* two of the rules
are checked, not whether:

- **Pre-trade checks** still reject a trade before it executes: account status,
  position-size and leverage caps, venue concentration, the rate limit, and a stale
  price feed.
- **Drawdown and daily loss** are checked **after the fill settles**: the system
  observes the real fill, recomputes your equity, and if you've crossed a limit, the
  account is **frozen** with the breach recorded. This is the honest way to enforce
  those two rules once a real venue is in the loop. The same model is described in
  [On-chain rule enforcement](/transparency/enforcement).

## If a funded account is terminated

A drawdown or daily-loss breach on a funded account **ends it**. There's no in-place
revival. You re-enter by **purchasing a new evaluation** under the same
[credential](/record/credential), at whatever tier you choose. Your credential keeps
your full history; the terminated account doesn't come back.

## What carries over from the beta

Everything you build now follows you in. Your [Genesis credential](/record/credential)
**evolves in place**: it gains real-capital history (funded passes, real P&L, funded
date, payout history) without ever being re-issued. The
[leaderboard](/record/leaderboard) and your [public profile](/record/profile) continue
straight through. The record you build in the closed beta is the foundation of your
funded standing.

## Tiers in the funded phase

The funded phase adds **Elite** ($100k) and **Whale** ($250k) above Pro, and attaches
a profit split to every tier. The full ladder is in
[Profit splits & scaling](/funded/splits).
