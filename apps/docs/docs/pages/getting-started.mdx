# Getting started

This page takes you from landing on the site to your first trade. The whole flow is
designed to take a few minutes. There is no document upload, no multi-day review,
and no crypto setup.

## 1. Create your account

Sign up with **email or a social login**. There is no wallet to install and no seed
phrase to write down. An account is provisioned for you in the background; you'll
see _"account created,"_ never _"wallet created."_ Crypto is the plumbing here; you
never have to touch it; see [Verifiability](/transparency/verifiability) if you want
to understand what's happening underneath.

:::info
During the **closed beta**, access is invite-led. Starter evaluations are opened via
an invite code; higher tiers are unlocked by paying the evaluation fee. See
[Fees & payment](/evaluations/payment).
:::

## 2. Choose a tier

Pick one of three evaluation tiers. Each sets how large your account is, how much
profit you need, and how much room you have to lose before the evaluation ends.

| Tier | Account size | Profit target | Max drawdown | Max leverage |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Starter** | $10,000 | 8% | 10% | 10× |
| **Basic** | $25,000 | 8% | 8% | 8× |
| **Pro** | $50,000 | 10% | 8% | 8× |

The full ruleset, including the daily loss limit and trade budget, is shown
before you commit. Read [Tiers & accounts](/evaluations/tiers) and
[The rules](/evaluations/rules) for the detail.

## 3. Place your first trade

You land in the **evaluation cockpit**, your trading workspace. From there you can:

- Browse the [markets](/evaluations/markets) and open a position long or short.
- See your exact fill (price, house tilt, and fee) **before** you submit.
- Attach a [take-profit and stop-loss bracket](/evaluations/trading) to manage risk.
- Watch your equity curve, drawdown gauge, daily-loss budget, and profit-target
  progress update in real time.

Every rule is live from your first trade. If a trade would put you past your
drawdown floor or daily loss limit, the evaluation ends the moment that breach is
detected. See [Passing & failing](/evaluations/outcomes).

## 4. Hit your target

Reach your tier's profit target with every rule intact and you **pass**. Passing
levels up your [Genesis credential](/record/credential), moves you up the
[leaderboard](/record/leaderboard), and unlocks the next tier.
